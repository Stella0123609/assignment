Hot air baloon

maasai Mara is best known for its scenic beauty.The famous Hot Air Balloon Safari, made us explore the landscape and birds chirping around which was sensational.This was the most surreal way to experience the wilderness of the Maasai Mara

migration

During the great migration, the grass is indeed greener on the other side. Each year, over two million wildebeast, zebra and other herbivores trek from the southern Serengeti to the lush green grasses of the Masai Mara. Known as one of the seven wonders of the world, the great migration is an iconic safari

Sunsets

Sundowners usually take place anywhere around the hills in the maasai mara reserve from 6pm.Drinks and snacks are served and there is entertainments from locals.Afer the sunsets a campfire is set up as people have fun.
